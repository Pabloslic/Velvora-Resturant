Citrus Gold Spritz

N5,500

Light, sparkling, and beautifully refreshing, the Citrus Gold Spritz combines orange blossom, citrus syrup, and chilled botanicals into a drink that brightens the table. It is crisp enough to cleanse the palate and refined enough to stand on its own.

Flavor

Zesty, floral, lightly sweet, and sparkling with a clean finish.

Best For

Brunches, warm evenings, seafood pairings, and guests who want something lively.

Served With

Orange blossom syrup, chilled citrus, and refreshing botanical notes.

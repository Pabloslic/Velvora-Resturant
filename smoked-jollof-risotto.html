Chef's Special

Smoked Jollof Risotto

N14,500

A bold Velvora favorite, this plate brings together the smoky soul of jollof and the creamy comfort of risotto. Slow-stirred rice absorbs layers of spice, stock, and gentle heat before being finished with parmesan crisp for texture and richness.

Flavor

Smoky, spicy, creamy, and deeply comforting with a vibrant West African finish.

Best For

Guests who love fusion dishes and rich rice plates with personality.

Served With

Parmesan crisp, herb oil, and a slow-cooked tomato-pepper base.
